High quality and quick tailor services

Make your life a little easier with Vlada Professional Tailoring! We offer high quality professional tailoring and alterations services at affordable prices.
Vlada Professional Tailoring & Alterations — family business of Ivan & Vlada Kovalyshyn. We started our professional way in Ukraine more than 20 years ago, now we're extending it in Calgary for you. We handicraft each garment to fulfill the highest level of expectations!

If you're not sure what you want, we'll help you to find the best outfit with regard to your individual features — body type, colours and fashion style. We will create clothing you'll love!

If you have clothing that you love, but size is a little bit wrong or your clothing needs to be repaired, our team of experienced tailors will provide the services you need to make your clothing fit perfectly. Just let us know!
